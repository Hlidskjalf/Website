Websites



Everlast Light

A commercial site for Everlast Lighting. A company owned and operated by Full Spectrum solutions, based in Jackson, MI. Originally the site was locally hosted by the parent company, but updates were required. The update project was to convert the site to a bootstrap site, built on the squarespace engine. Overall the project went smoothly, and was a vast improvement. The updated site can be viewed here.



Full Spectrum Solutions

Full Spectrum Solutions, a residential lighting company based in Jackson, MI. This site is a nightmare to update, and with a fully online company it is a complete embarassment. The site is hosted and built with SearchFit, and requires constant maintenance. A project was created to update and overhaul the site, and never went through because the current version of the site was built by the CEO of the company. Fearing change, and refusing to update his creation, this site stands as a testament to pride and continues to cost the company money and sales. The current version, over 10 years old, can be viewed here.



Previous versions of this site
